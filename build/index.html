Why choose us?

Over 65 million people worldwide are suffering from incurable neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer’s Disease (AD), Parkinson’s Disease (PD), Huntington’s Disease (HD), Multiple Sclerosis (MS), Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), etc. Unfortunately, the cause of these diseases is not completely understood, therefore there is still no cure to counteract or stop disease evolution. Big pharmaceutical companies, research groups and biotech companies are rushing to find a cure and therefore save lives of millions. New research data is published every day supplying fresh knowledge, thus aiding in revealing the pathophysiology and source of the diseases. It is very complex to grasp and gather all the new information. We use Machine Learning (ML) for data acquisition and database update on various neurodegenerative diseases. The collected big data serves as a solid basement for drug development and improvement used by research groups and biotech startup companies.
